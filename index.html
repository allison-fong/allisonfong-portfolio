About

My name is Allison Fong (she/her). I am a designer and rising junior at Washington University in St. Louis' Sam Fox School of Design & Visual Arts pursuing a major in Communication Design with double minors in Human-Computer Interaction and American Culture Studies.

My interests range from graphic design, product design to creative coding and emerging technologies. I strive to find balance and seek new challenges that scratches all of my itches ranging from creativity to technical. I am passionate about uplifting diverse perspectives and advocating for disability inclusion and empowerment within my spaces.

Design's ability to positively influence behaviors, lifestyles, and communities propels my work; my practice is rooted in the belief that it can be utilized for bringing communities together and creating a positive impact. I also want to integrate a human-centered approach to leverage creativity and innovation for sustainable community empowerment.
